About this project

The Mastercraft Bamboo monitor Riser is a sturdy and stylish platform that elevate your screen to a more comfortable viewing heihgt. Placing your monitor at eye level has the potential to improve your posture and makes you more comfortable while at work, helping you stay focus on the task at hand.

Featuring artisans craftmanship, the simplicity of design creates extra desk space below your computer to allo notepads,pens, and USB sticks to be stored under the stand.
